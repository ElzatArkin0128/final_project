What is Count?

Do you want to be in control of your own carbon footprint? The Count app will help you make simple and easy changes to become more environmentally friendly. The app will let you know what part of your lifestyle is making a bigger impact, and gives you simple tips to lessen your carbon footprint.

Good luck on your eco-friendly journey!
