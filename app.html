The Counter app

The app helps the user get a good understanding of their own CO2 budget, and motivate them to cut their own emissions. It's easy to use, educational and a fun interactive app. We will help you set up realistic goals. We give you personalized advice, tips and tricks on how reach those goals and motivate you when it feels like your they are out of reach. We reward you when you do good, so it will be easier to sucess!

Create your own account

When the user first opens the app after downloading, you will be met with a log in interface with the option to register an account.

Add shopping lists

Plan ahead and create your own shopping list, receiving detailed information of how much CO2 your chosen products will create.

Calculator function

The app offers a CO2 emission calculator that allows the user to register every impact your footprint leaves behind. Registering travels, clothing, food, and other products gives the user an accurate calculation of how much CO2 they are using. This function helps the user understand and raise awareness about how different decisions will lead to different CO2 amounts.

Get personalized advice

Based on each individual user’s CO2 emissions, the app offers fun and personal advice that are based on the user’s habits. Maybe you are using your car more than you need? Take the bus!

Stay on track with your calender

By tracking consumption and other CO2 consuming habits in the calendar function, it is easy to keep track of improvements. It allows the user to have a overview of how their personal CO2 emissions are either increasing or decreasing in different parts of their lives.

Get rewards when you accomplish your goals

Of course: cutting your CO2 emissions should come from internal motivation, but for further motivation the app even offers a rewarding system based on how much you have improved your lifestyle. The rewards differ from discounts at chosen stores, to eco-friendly products that will keep helping the user to improve their habits.
