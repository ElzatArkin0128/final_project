The Count app

The count app helps you to be more concerned with your CO2 footprint in your everyday life. It's an easy to use, entertaining and interactive app. You can calculate your CO2 emissions from your shopping list, get personalized advices to improve your CO2 footprint, fix your own goals and store everything on the calendar.

Create your own account

When the user first opens the app after downloading, you will be met with a log in interface with the option to register an account.

Calculate your CO2 emissions based on your shopping list

Plan ahead and create your own shopping list, receiving detailed information of how much CO2 your chosen products will create.

Calculator function

The app offers a CO2 emission calculator that allows the user to register every impact your footprint leaves behind. Registering travels, clothing, food, and other products gives you an accurate calculation of how much CO2 you are worth, also helping raise awareness of how to reduce the emissions.

Get personalized advices

Based on each individual user’s CO2 emissions, the app offers fun and customized advices that are based on the user’s habits. Maybe you are using your car more than you need? Take the bus!

Save everything on your calender

By tracking consumption and other CO2 consuming habits in the calendar function, it is easy to keep track of improvements. It allows the user to have a overview of how their personal CO2 emissions are either increasing or decreasing in different parts of their lives.

Get rewards when you accomplish your goals

Of course: cutting your CO2 emissions should come from internal motivation, but for further motivation the app even offers a rewarding system based on how much you have improved your lifestyle. The rewards differ from discounts at chosen stores, to eco-friendly products that will keep helping the user to improve their habits.
