Sustainable Developement Goals

The UN tells us that Climate Change is a global challenge that affects everyone, everywhere. Their sustainable development goal number 13: Climate Action, urges us to take action to help us reach our climate goals. Their researches have set a goal of limiting global climate change to 1,5* degrees celsius.

Why Count is a step in the right direction?

Many of us are interested in climate change, and want to make choices that are good for the climate. But which choices are good climate choices? Its not easy to know. Thats why we made Co2unter, to help users see how much CO2 they use in their day to day life, and to help them make good choices. The app helps the user get a good understanding of their own CO2 budget, and motivate them to cut their own emissions.

When people use our app, they get an understanding of how much CO2 they use in a specific time frame. While using the app they will notice how buying product A vs product B will impact their CO2 use differently, and then next time they buy something they are more likely to choose the more sustainable option. The goal is to make behavior change easy for our users, and promote climate positive change.

Count is a very measurable app, our users can see exactly how much CO2 they use. The positives on seeing black on white how much your emmissions change, is that the users get a feeling of actually making a difference. They can see that they have gotten better than last month, which motivates them and helps them get better.
