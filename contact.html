Our Team

The co-creators of Count met as students at Oslo Metropolitan University. After years of group projects, final exams, and other projects besides their studies, they developed a professional chemistry and competence which they further utilized when developing Count. Bringing in experience from different industries the team managed to create a diverse team, including competence within economy, arts, pedagogy, start-ups, and green energy.

Olöf Jónsdóttir | CEO

OlofJ@co2unt.com
Olöf understood that this team was highly motivated to do good things for the climate. We came up with the idea for Count after noticing that it was very hard to understand how much CO2 each of us were using. We noticed that applications like Gmail were able to see when you had bought a flight, and automatically added the flight dates to your calendar. Therefore it couldn´t be that hard to automatically send all your reciepts to a Co2 calculator? And then Co2unt was born.
